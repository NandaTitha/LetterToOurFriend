Dear Baby Blue,

Di bawah ini surat real, tolong dibaca dengan seksama dan serius.

The statement Geo said, "untung ada Baby Blue," has its realest meaning. No gimmick, no perez. In this world people come and go, and I'm truly grateful that I have Baby Blue to go through all the things that happen.

Untung ada Baby Blue, so I have friends to be jamet with, to do gimmick with, and the biggest reason for me to go back to Cirebon.

You've been there since all the drama I had back then in senior high school. Watching me "menangis jelek" because of that little bolu ketan, and fighting all the things that caused it. After that, in the covid season, thank you for still sparing time for the little catch-ups, and for being one reason to go out of the house.

As you guys know, I don't have a huge reason to go to Cirebon anymore. Even my home doesn't really feel like home. But going home for you guys always makes it worth the trip.

Thank you for being a good team since we did all the crazy things to make our lives better in SHS. Les Bu Neni, foto soal ulangan, all the nyontek things.

Before going to the main session of this letter, I would like to say thank you from the deepest part of my heart. Thank you for staying, and for always having a proker so we can keep in touch. Thank you for giving me a safe space to gosip, to ask, and to be the realest version of myself.

Maybe you'll say this is "ala-ala banget," but I think just in this moment I'll say it. Once again, thank you for always being my happy pill since senior high school. I hope you guys are good every day and happy every day. Let God give you all the blessings He has, and create happy moments for you, just as you've always been my happy pill. Because you guys are all the love story I have.

Now, the story continues to something bigger. Since 2025, Nanda and I decided to take this relationship further. Despite all the drama, I'm truly sure that Nanda is the one, and he has all the things I need. With him, I can feel what home feels like.

Back then in 2016, we went to Bali for study tour. Hopefully this time, 10 years later, you go to Bali to witness my moment.

With this letter, I sincerely invite you to be there again, in one of the biggest moments of my life, to become one with the person I've chosen.

I know it's far. I know it will take a lot of your time. But you know, kehadiran kalian will really make my day more complete. Please take this as my special invitation for you guys. That phrase that says "tiada kesan tanpa kehadiranmu" is really meant.

And above all, I hope you can hold Nanda and me in your thoughts, and send a little prayer for the plans we are building together.

With love, Titha 🤍
